My Passions & Hobbies

🎮 Gaming

From competitive shooters to immersive RPGs, gaming is my favorite escape. Since I was a young lad, gaming has always been a passion, ever since I was first introduced to it at the age of 5. Going to gaming cafes with my brother and friends is a core memory of my childhood.

⚽ FC Barcelona

My favourite football team is FC Barcelona. A team based in Barcelona, Spain. The club's motto "Més Que Un Club", meaning more than a club, is what best describes it; It signifies that the institution represents far more than just football, embodying Catalan identity and democratic ownership by its members.

🎣 Fishing & Hunting

Since childhood, I have loved the outdoors. My dad would take me fishing and since then its been my a passion. Just being able to take in nature and the quietness. Even if I get Skunked, its still enjoyable.

🛸 Conspiracy Theories

Diving down the rabbit hole to question everything we know. This is a bad guilty pleasue, because nowadays everything is one. However, the past conspiracy theories have turned out to be true. Will the ones that are now be also true in the future? thats what grips me
